Why tips about guitars?

I've always liked guitars and have been into playing for a while. Recently, I bought a new electric guitar which is a replica of Jimi Hendrix's guitar!

This is what my guitar looks like.

I first started playing when I was younger but due to moving and my short attention span, I dropped learning it. However, after years, I want to try again.

Being in a new environment, I realized I need something to ground myself and not stress so much about work. I feel learning guitar can be a healthy outlet for stress and creativity that everyone should at least try if they're interested.

Special thanks to my boss, she let me take a couple days off to work on this project
