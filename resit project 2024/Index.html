When first purchasing a guitar, you should research what type of music you'd like to play!

Types of guitars?

There are three main types of guitars: the acoustic guitar, which uses its hollow body to produce the deep sound we hear; the electric guitar, which uses six magnets to pick up the vibrations, allowing you to play in several different types of tones; and the bass guitar, which creates a deep, supporting sound to complement the rest of the instruments being played.

This is a photo of my new guitar! When buying a guitar second-hand, make sure there aren't any cracks on the body. Another thing to look out for is old, used strings; it's always best to replace the old ones if they seem worn, and always have a spare just in case, especially when you're just starting to learn!
